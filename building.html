How to Build Your Gaming PC

Building a gaming pc is all about gathering all your parts and assembling them together into your pc chassis(case).

Step 1: Case

Have your case open and laid down for assembling.

Step 2: Motherboard

Start with your motherboard. Before putting anything into the case start with your motherboard outside your case. On the mother board you can start inserting your cpu, ram, and storage.

step 3: Cpu

Insert the cpu. The cpu is a small square chip that inserts into a square on the motherboard. Depending on if you have an intel or amd cpu the pins on the cpu will either be on the chip or on the motherboard. However applying it is the same process. First locate the small triangle on one of your cpu's four corners and line it up with the triangle on the board. No pressure is needed to put the cpu into the board, just simply drop it into place and pull down the lever to lock it into place.

step 4: Ram

Insering the ram. This part is very straighforward. Follow your motherboard's manual to see which slots your ram sticks should go into. Then just lift the lever apply some pressure and they should push right into the slots. These only go in one way, so you can't mess it up.
