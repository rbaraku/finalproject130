Gaming pc intro

Gaming PC's are the elite level of today's gaming. They are the high end gaming systems and it is due to the extremely powerful specs that they can hold. Gaming PC's are bigger, stronger, and faster than any console or other gaming system. Their capacity of power takes them to a category all on their own. With Gaming PC's being stronger and faster than any other gaming console, they also come at a much higher price, but when experiencing the level of gaming that it provides, it doesn't take long to see that the price is worth it. Here we are going to provide a detailed overview on Gaming PC parts and some steps on how to build one yourself.

Components

MotherBoard

The main part of any computer is the motherboard. This circuit board is essentially the platform of the computer. It is where all the components and peripherals connect to and it helps communicate and connect everything.

CPU

The central processing unit is essentially the brain of the computer. Without this, the computer can't function and can't tell any of it's parts what to do. It is usually a small square with many pins or pin slots on it that sets onto the motherboard.
